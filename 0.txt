Logical ds are implemented using any of the Physical ds.
Logical ds tells how to perform operations on physical ds or stored data.

Logical ds are mainly of 3 types:
1. Linear DS
   i). stack        - works on LIFO
  ii). queues       - works on FIFO

2. Non Linear DS
   i). trees
  ii). graph

3. may be Linear or may be Tabular DS
  i). hash table
